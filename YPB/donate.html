Donate now, support the work of YPB in West Africa and give people in fragile and conflict affected areas a chance!!

Almost a quarter of the world’s population lives in poverty. They are the most vulnerable to natural disasters, war and violence. YPB wants everybody to have the chance to live a safe and worthy life, with access to healthcare, education and economic opportunities. With over 15 years of experience, YPB has a leading role in innovative and effective forms of development in West Africa.

Promoting Tourism

Young Peace Brigades creates opportunities for West Africa’s poorest, most vulnerable and excluded people. Regardless of race or religion, YPB provides African citizens and Youths with an opportunity to make their voices heard.

Join 0ver 2,000 volunteers across West Africa who believe we can build flourishing communities together, even in the most fragile or conflict-ridden areas.

In flourishing communities people share values, interests and a common purpose. Flourishing communities create space where people can live with dignity, safely, have the freedom to speak out and have access to quality and affordable basic services, such as healthcare.

Young Peace Brigades

Working with all to promote peace and sustainable empowerment of communities across Ghana.

Young people under 25 are the majority of the population in fragile
and conflict-affected societies.

However are involved in preventing violence, countering violent extremism,
transforming conflict and building peace in their communities.
But their work often lacks recognition and support.

Our programme

YPB promotes and advocates for peace, conflict transformation and management while working with institutions and other civil society groups to prevent and counter violent extremism by promoting a rights based approach.
We also engage in improving health, education and economic empowerment through this programmes.
